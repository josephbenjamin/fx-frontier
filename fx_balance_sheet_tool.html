How Scenario Analysis Works

The analysis tests every possible way your net debt could be distributed across the selected currencies. The step size controls the granularity: at 10% steps, each currency can hold 0%, 10%, 20%… up to 100% of total debt. Every combination of these values that sums to 100% becomes a distinct scenario — together they form a complete, exhaustive map of the debt currency space.

Each scenario is then run through the Monte Carlo FX engine, which simulates thousands of possible future exchange rate paths drawn from a log-normal model calibrated to your spot, forward and volatility inputs, with correlated moves across currencies. The resulting distributions of equity change and leverage ratio are summarised for every scenario, letting you identify which debt currency mixes offer the greatest resilience to adverse FX moves.
